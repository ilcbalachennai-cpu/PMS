Comprehensive User Manual • v03.01.03

Welcome to BharatPay Pro, a premium Payroll Management System designed for precision, security, and ease of use. This manual will guide you through every aspect of the software, including the advanced Multi-Company Architecture introduced in the V03 series.

1. Getting Started

1.1 First-Time Installation

When you launch BharatPay Pro for the first time, the system will initialize your local environment and hardware binding.

  1. Grant Permissions: If Windows Firewall or Anti-Virus prompts you, click "Allow" to ensure the local database and update services can run.
  2. Registration: New users must click "Register Now".
    • Identity Verification: Enter the OTP sent to your registered Email/Mobile to proceed.
    • Set Password: Create a strong Administrator Password. This is tied to your hardware; do not lose it.
Hardware Binding: Your installation is locked to your specific machine. If you need to move data to a new computer, use the Identity Restoration portal in the Login screen.

1.2 Daily Sync & Security

  • Internet Synchronization: The system requires an internet connection daily to verify license status and refresh cloud security tokens.
  • Idle Timeout: For security, the app will automatically log you out after 10 minutes of inactivity.

2. Multi-Organization Management

BharatPay Pro V03 supports managed multiple establishments or independent companies within a single installation. Each unit operates in its own **Data Silo** for total isolation.

2.1 The Organization Selector (Gate)

If you have multiple organizations registered, the system will present the Organization Selector immediately after login. Select the unit you wish to work on for the current session.

Smart Load: If only one establishment is registered, the system will bypass the selector and load your Dashboard automatically.

2.2 Adding & Switching Units

  • Add New Unit: Use the "Add New Establishment" button in the selector screen to register a second or third company.
  • Live Switching: While in the Dashboard, use the Company Switcher in the sidebar to jump between units without logging out.

3. Statutory Compliance Configuration

3.1 Default Basis (Code Wages)

By default, Code Wages (Labour Code compliant) serve as the basis for all statutory calculations, including EPF, ESI, and Gratuity. The system automatically monitors if exclusions exceed the 50% threshold and adjusts contributions accordingly.

3.2 Initial Configuration

Configure the statutory compliance settings to match your establishment's specific state and center requirements:

  • Core Compliance: Setup EPF, ESI, Professional Tax (PT slabs vary by state), and Labour Welfare Fund (LWF).
  • Policies: Define your Gratuity, Leave Policy (EL/SL/CL), and Bonus Policy.
  • Optional Modules: Choose to enable or disable Overtime (OT) and Arrear Salary calculations.

4. Setting Up Your Company

Before adding employees, configure your establishment profile in Settings > Company Profile.

  1. Establishment Details: Enter Name, Address, PAN, GST, PF, and ESI codes.
  2. Statutory Configuration: Set ceilings and PT/LWF cycles as per your local jurisdiction.

5. Employee Management

Navigate to the Employees section to manage your workforce master data.

  • Add Employee: Capture DOJ, Bank Details, and Statutory UAN/IP numbers.
  • Bulk Import: Use the "Import Excel" feature to migrate large datasets instantly.
  • Leave Ledgers: Automated tracking for EL (Earned Leave), SL (Sick Leave), and CL (Casual Leave).
  • Documents: Upload photos and digital copies of statutory forms for each employee.

6. Monthly Payroll Workflow

The "Pay Process" module follows a streamlined, audit-ready workflow:

  1. Attendance: Enter "Present Days". LOP is calculated automatically.
  2. Leave Management: Record leaves availed with real-time balance validation.
  3. Advance & Fines: Automated EMI recovery until the loan balance is zero.
  4. Overtime (OT): Capture OT hours/days based on your enabled policy.
  5. Arrear Salary: Retroactive revisions with Effective Month tracking and ad-hoc or percentage logic.
  6. Processing: Click "Calculate Salaries" to compute the entire stack instantly.
  7. Safety Backup: A "Safety Snapshot" of your database is created automatically before finalized updates or major changes.

7. Reporting & Analytics

7.1 Pay Reports

  • Pay Sheet: Detailed summary filterable by Site, Branch, or Division.
  • Pay Slips: Professional slips for bulk distribution or direct mailing.
  • Bank Statement: Ready-to-use transfer instructions with accounts and IFSC codes.

7.2 Statutory Filings

  • PF (Provident Fund): PF ECR (Text/Excel), Arrear ECR, and forms (12A, 3A, 6A).
  • ESI: Monthly Return (Excel), Form 5, and Joiner/Leaver lists.
  • Labor Registers: Form B (Wage Register) and Form C (Muster Roll) compliant with current laws.

7.3 MIS & Analysis

  • Dynamic Report Builder: Build custom spreadsheets with user-selected columns.
  • Increment Analysis: Compare salary growth and variance between periods.
  • Mailing Service: Bulk delivery of encrypted payslips to employee emails.

8. Code Analysis (Social Security)

Prepare for the new Social Security Code 2020 with simulation tools:

  • Clause 88 Threshold: Automatic monitoring of the 50% exclusion limit.
  • Impact Simulation: Projected liability comparisons under "Code Wages" vs "Original Wages".
  • Coverage Monitoring: Analysis of ceiling impacts on employee eligibility.

9. Legacy Data Migration

If you are upgrading from BharatPay Pro V2 (Single Company) to V3 (Multi-Company), follow these steps to migrate your legacy data securely:

  1. Initialization: Launch V03.01.03 and complete the registration for your primary establishment unit.
  2. Access Data Management: Navigate to Utilities > Data Management from the main dashboard of your new unit.
  3. Restore Legacy Backup: Click "Restore Data" and select your existing legacy backup file (usually .enc or .sqlite).
  4. Automated Mapping: The V3 engine will automatically detect the legacy format and map your existing employees, masters, and payroll history into the new company-scoped ID system.
Note: Migration only needs to be performed once for each legacy establishment you wish to manage in the new multi-unit environment.

10. Data Management Functions

BharatPay Pro provides advanced data maintenance tools to ensure your establishment records remain healthy and secure. Each function is strictly scoped to the active establishment only.

10.1 Backup Data

Creates a high-security encrypted snapshot (.enc) of the active establishment's database. It is recommended to perform a backup before every payroll finalization.

10.2 Restore Data

Imports an existing backup into the current establishment silo. This will overwrite the current unit's data with the contents of the backup. Use this for data recovery or migration.

10.3 Partial Payroll Reset

Clears only the payroll results for the current active month. This allows you to start the monthly processing from scratch if errors are detected, without affecting your Employee Master or previous months' history.

10.4 Factory Reset (Company Scope)

Wipes **all** data associated with the current establishment (Employees, Payroll History, and Settings). This action is permanent and requires the Administrator Password. Other establishments in your installation remain unaffected.

10.5 Purge System Logs

Cleans the background activity logs and temporary calculation files. Regular purging helps maintain high application performance during complex payroll runs.

11. Troubleshooting

  • "Security Violation": Reset system clock to "Internet Time" and restart.
  • "License Locked": Ensure internet connectivity or use the Identity Restoration portal.
  • "Sync Required": Triggered after 24 hours offline. Connect for 60 seconds to refresh.
